Reading List

Modern Software Engineering

There is much the data practitioner can gain from study of Software Engineering, especially as it is presented in this book.

The two central requirements of effective Software Engineering are extremely practical for anyone working with data:
- to become expert at learning
- to become expert at managing complexity

I picked up this book after finding Dave Farley on YouTube. The content is tight, compelling, and manges to be suitably theoretical as well as practical.
